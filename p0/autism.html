Autism

Co-occurring Conditions

Many Individual with autism also experience other conditions such as epilepsy (affecting up to 30% of autistic individuals), anxiety, ADHD, or depression.

Not Caused By Vaccines

Numerous studies have debunked the myth that vaccines cause autism. Research indicates that autism is linked to genetic and enviromental factors.

Sensory Processing Challenges

People with Autism often struggle with sensory processing, leading to heightened sensitivity to sounds, textures, or lights, which can sometimes result in sensory overload.

Exceptional Abilities

Many people with autism have exceptional skills in areas like music, math, art, or memorization

Early Signs and Diagnosis

Early intervention services can significantly improve outcomes for individuals with autism.

Spectrum Disorder

Autism is a spectrum disorder, meaning it affects individuals differently and to varying degrees.

Gender Differences

Boys are about 4 times more likely to be diagnosed with autism than girls

Prevalence

Autism affects approximately 1 in 36 children in the United States, according to the latest CDC report.
