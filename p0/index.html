Parenting Neurodivergent Kids

Welcome!

This is a site all about parenting your neurodivergent kiddo. Neurodivergent is an umbrella term, it includes things like ADHD, Autism, OCD, and more.

We know Neurodivergence

Learning

Learning about neurodivergence involves understanding that individuals' brains function differently, often in unique and valuable ways, and recognizing these differences as natural variations rather than deficits.

Implementing

Implementing knowledge about neurodivergence means creating inclusive environments that accommodate diverse thinking styles and needs, fostering acceptance and support for all individuals.

Teaching

Teaching neurodivergent children involves implementing diverse strategies such as visual supports, structured environments, multisensory learning approaches, and assistive technologies to accommodate their unique needs and learning styles.

Loving

Loving a neurodivergent child means embracing their unique strengths, accepting their challenges, and providing unconditional support and understanding to help them thrive in their own way.
